About Us

In this post-truth, austerity led and Trump infested era the world can sometimes feel a very lonely place.

BuddyUp wants to change that.

Started by three friends from London in 2017, BuddyUp was born out of the desire to make the world a friendlier, more welcoming place for all.

By connecting welcoming locals with enthusiastic and inquisitive newbies, in 7 cities across the world, BuddyUp makes every trip away feel like coming home.

Want to know where the best place to grab a beer? Or the prettiest spot to view the night lights? Or the freshest club to the dance the night away in?

BuddyUp's squad has got your back!

In true BuddyUp spirit, if you ever happen to be in the area, then be sure to come give us a visit at 1 Canada Square. We make a mean cup of tea (and even have Jammy Dodgers!)
